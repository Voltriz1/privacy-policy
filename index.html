Summary: QuickCurrency is a currency conversion utility. We do not require user registration and we do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or password for the app’s core currency conversion function. The app may use third-party advertising SDKs in supported versions of the app.

1. Information We Collect and Process

QuickCurrency itself does not collect or store the following for its core features:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or postal address
  • Password, account credentials, or contact list
  • Bank account or payment card information

To provide app functionality and advertising support, the app or integrated third-party SDKs may process limited device-related information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers and device identifiers
  • Approximate or precise location information
  • Device, network, and connection status
  • Installed app visibility or app availability checks used for deep links and SDK compatibility

2. App Functionality Data

The app uses an internet connection to request exchange rates from third-party API providers. These requests are used only to provide currency conversion results. We also store your selected theme, language, time format, and currency preferences locally on your device.

3. Advertising Services

QuickCurrency may use third-party advertising services depending on the app version and distribution channel, including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Huawei Ads Kit / Petal Ads

These services may process device-related information for ad delivery, advertising measurement, anti-fraud protection, regional compliance, deep-link handling, and service compatibility.

4. Permissions

The app may request or include permissions such as Internet access, network state, advertising ID access, approximate location, precise location, Bluetooth-related access, phone/network state, and installed-app visibility. Some of these permissions are required by integrated advertising SDKs rather than the core currency conversion feature itself.

5. Disclosure to Third Parties

We may disclose limited device-related data to third-party service providers only as necessary to provide exchange-rate data and advertising services. This includes API providers and advertising SDK providers such as Google and Huawei. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data Storage and Security

Data transmission for exchange-rate requests and advertising services is performed over encrypted network connections where supported. Preference settings are stored locally on your device. QuickCurrency does not maintain user accounts or store personal identity data on its own servers for the core app experience.

7. Children's Privacy

QuickCurrency is a general-purpose utility application. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app’s core functionality.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You can manage some advertising and privacy preferences through your device settings. You may also choose to stop using the app at any time and remove locally stored preferences by uninstalling the app.
